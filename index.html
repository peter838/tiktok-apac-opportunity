Lowest-Hanging Fruit

Where to start: recurring, easy-conversion categories

These five product families combine non-discretionary demand, predictable replacement cycles, and proven purchase patterns across the APAC portfolio. They convert faster than project-based or event-driven categories.

☕
Office & Pantry Supplies
Recurring

Non-discretionary, monthly recurring, high purchase frequency. Every site buys paper, pantry stock, and washroom consumables regardless of budget cycles.

Market signalAlready top-3 observed in CN, JP, AU, MY, ID. Easy to expand share.
🎧
IT Accessories & Equipment
Refresh cycle

Headsets, monitors, keyboards have predictable refresh cycles (24-36 months). Demand scales with headcount.

Market signalStrong growth in CN (35K→192K). Collapse in ID (58K→10K) = reactivation target.
🖨️
Printer consumables (toner, cartridges)
Hidden demand

Sits inside Office or IT budgets but is a distinct, recurring purchase. Logistics environments still print heavily (manifests, labels, invoices).

Market signalHidden inside Office & Pantry line; usually under-counted.
👞
Safety shoes, PPE & Uniforms
Compliance

Compliance-driven, annual replacement cycle for frontline staff. Not optional — regulated in logistics.

Market signalObserved uniforms spend is near-zero in most markets. Likely routed elsewhere or unsupplied.
🔧
MRO consumables & services
Operational

Conveyor belts, dock equipment, facility maintenance. Operational necessity with fragmented buying patterns.

Market signalAU spiked to 679K (2024); CN ramped 1K→147K. Real demand exists.
